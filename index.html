Why The Pet Parent Blueprint exists

Most pet parents assume someone is checking these products before they are sold.

That seems reasonable.

In reality, many pet products are sold without ingredient limits, material testing, or meaningful pre-market safety review unless they make a medical claim.

This is how things like lead in some ceramic bowls, BPA in certain plastics, flame retardants in pet beds, and undisclosed ingredients in grooming products can still end up on store shelves.

The problem is not a lack of safe options.

The problem is not knowing how to tell which products are safe and which could be harmful.

This is why The Pet Parent Blueprint was created.
